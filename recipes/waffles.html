Waffle Recipe

Make these yummy waffles in under 10 minutes! Light and fluffy yet somehow perfectly crispy, these 5 star waffles are sure to keep you coming back for more! Serve them with maple syrup and some strips of streaky bacon and they are sure to be the "go to" sunday morning recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 ½ cups warm milk
  • ⅓ cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
